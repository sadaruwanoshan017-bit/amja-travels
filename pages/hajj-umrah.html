Questions

What is included in the Hajj package?

Our Hajj package includes return airfare from Colombo, 5-star accommodation in Zam Zam Tower (zero mileage to Haram), three-time daily buffet meals, A/C transport throughout, guided Ziyarah visits to Makkah and Madinah, and accompaniment by experienced Ulama from Sri Lanka for the entire duration.

How do I register for Hajj or Umrah through Amja Travels?

Contact us by phone (+94 11 233 5657 or +94 77 730 8079), email (amjatrvl@sltnet.lk) or visit our office at No. 30, Dias Place, Colombo 12. Our team will guide you through all documentation, government approvals and payment requirements step by step.

When do Umrah groups depart from Sri Lanka?

We schedule Umrah departures monthly throughout the year. Special Ramadan packages are also available during the holy month. Contact us to find the next available group departure that suits your schedule.

What documents are required for Hajj and Umrah?

Generally you will need a valid Sri Lankan passport (6+ months validity), NIC, 4 recent passport photographs and a completed application form. For Hajj, additional registration with the government Hajj authority is required. Our team handles the complete documentation process on your behalf.

Is the Hajj package price inclusive of everything?

Our advertised price of Rs. 945,000 covers flights, accommodation, meals, transport and Ulama guidance. Certain personal expenses such as souvenirs, laundry and optional activities are not included. We are completely transparent — no hidden charges. Contact us for a full breakdown before you commit.
